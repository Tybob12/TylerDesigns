Decade Counter

The Circuit

The circuit was designed in PCB Wizard. This circuit has 2 sections. The first is a clock that pulses once every second. The second part is a counter that will use the pulses formed by the clock to display the letters of a word in sequential order. The pulse order of this circuit allows a 10 letter word or combination of 10 letters.
